##### Abstract

Stochastic differential equations are useful for modeling the prices of financial assets. In the present work a model for its study is proposed, until now not registered in the literature, called Mean Reverting Model with Stochastic Volatility. A methodology for estimating the parameters is developed using Euler’s numerical approximation scheme and the weak convergence of the GARCH processes to a stochastic differential equation. Conditions are established to model a temporary series of prices through this process. The results of the estimation are applied to electricity prices in Colombia and the quality of the adjustment of the model performed is checked. All the necessary simulations and algorithms were implemented in MATLAB (2015)
